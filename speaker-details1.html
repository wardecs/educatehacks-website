Damian Musk

Guest Speaker

AI Ethics Presentation

Event Date/Time: April 8th, 9:30 PM EST

  • Click Here for Zoom Link

    • Artificial intelligence is not itself a new concept in technology: in fiction, writers having been exploring superintelligence going off the rails for decades. Yet, in the advent of neural networks, AI is progressing far faster than ever before. As we incorporate increasing sophisticated neural networks into our society and education systems, this means we're going to have to think about the ethical ramifications of what we're doing as well as what AI means for changes in society as a whole. To do this, we'll also examine how current educational technology currently does—or doesn’t—employ an AI-ethical mindset in the pursuit of a new era of teaching.

    There will be an opportunity for Q&A with the presenter
